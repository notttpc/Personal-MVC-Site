Anime Dungeon App

  • Category: C# Language
  • Project date: July, 2023

Application Description

A battle-simulated PVI where the player is given choices of pre-made characters and weapons. They are allowed to go back and forth between weapons and characters. Any wrong key inputs gives the user a second chance to pick a character/weapon. In the game's main screen, you can see the 1/3 monster's stats that you are currently fighting and your character/weapon stats. The player's health is updated all throughout the gameplay and you can check your health after every fight. You can continue to keep fighting till you die or choose to leave on your own.
